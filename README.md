## 💡 Frequently asked questions

**Does the app need an internet connection?**
Yes. The app needs the internet to verify your settings and perform tasks.

**Can I run multiple tasks at once?**
The app handles one task at a time to ensure stability. Wait for a task to finish before you start another one.

**Where do my logs go?**
The app saves logs in the Documents folder under mhr-ggate/logs. These files help if you encounter an error.

**Is my data private?**
The app runs on your computer. Your files stay on your machine at all times. The software does not send your data to outside servers.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting common issues

**The app does not open.**
Restart your computer. If the problem persists, uninstall the app through the Windows Settings menu and perform a clean install.

**The app runs slow.**
Close other applications that use high amounts of memory or processor power.

**I get an error during installation.**
Ensure you have administrative rights on your computer. Check that your antivirus does not block the software.

**The app shows a blank screen.**
Check if your graphics driver needs an update. Visit the website for your computer manufacturer to find the latest driver.
